About

Swiggy is a new-age consumer-first organization offering an easy-to-use convenience platform, accessible through a unified app. Swiggy is a revolutionary powerhouse transforming India's food delivery and hyperlocal commerce sectors, bringing unmatched convenience to urban living. Since its inception in 2014, Swiggy has blazed a trail in the food delivery industry, quickly becoming a household name known for its reliability and cutting-edge innovation. Our mission is simple yet profound: to elevate the quality of life for urban consumers by making convenience our core mantra and the driving force behind every decision we make. In 2020, Swiggy once again raised the bar with the launch of Quick Commerce, offering lightning-fast deliveries that cater to every need, from daily groceries to gourmet meals.

At the heart of Swiggy are our core values, the backbone of our culture, guiding us through every challenge and triumph.
